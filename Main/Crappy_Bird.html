A FlappyBird clone I made in some spare study periods.
So now you can play it on a computer!

Features

    - Saves highscores

    - Saves deaths

    - Addition of parallax backgrounds

    - Addition of scrolling clouds

    - Quick replay (single keypress)

    - Everything keyboard controlled
