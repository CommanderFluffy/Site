Info
In Search Of Darkness is a “Thomas Was Alone” clone, as soon as I saw this I wanted to know how it worked because it was such a simple graphical style yet it was so pretty.

Especially the dynamic shadows.


If you are unaware of the game mechanics of “Thomas Was Alone” basically you play as a group of rectangles and your objective is to use these differently shaped rectangles work together to get them to their respective exits.

The original was created in “Unity” but I don’t have any experience with that environment so instead I am using my main language, Java  so my version doesn’t give the performance the original provides and the fact that I’m not the greatest in terms of actually making my code fast.


Features (So far)

  1. -Map and properties are loaded from txt files, properties do not have to be in particular order

  2. -Up to 6 playable characters on screen

  3. -Some visual effects in the background and foreground like smoke on the water

  4. -Dynamic shadows

  5. -Level can be scaled, rotated and moved without effecting gameplay

  6. -Modular menu with animations

  7. -In game rendered text
