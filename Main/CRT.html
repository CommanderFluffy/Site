Features
This is my final project from my highschool course.
Its an attempt to create a retro style CRT display with some classic games such as Hangman, Pong, and Asteroids.


In hangman you can play against the computer in which the computer selects from a list of words, or another human in which you take turns entering and guessing words.
You can also add custom words to the dictionary which will be saved.

Pong can be played against the computer or another human.

Asteroids is one player only.


The controls are pretty simple, pressing left shift anywhere throughout the program will bring up a context based help which shows controls and other information.










































Download
This comes as a .jar, just double click to launch it.

Note: this works on OSX and Windows
