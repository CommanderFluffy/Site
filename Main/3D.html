Ever since I started programming 3D graphics have been on my mind, they have always seemed like some kind of magic trick to me. Trying to use OpenGL only made my confusion worse.

However learning higher level maths at uni has made everything a little clearer, at least to the point where I could understand the basic principles of the matter. This inspired me to actually go ahead and apply some of the algebra that I had learnt and thus this very basic little software 3D engine was born.


So far all its really capable of is rotation, translation, and scaling of wireframes. But hopefully I’ll eventually come back and try to implement solid sides, lighting, and all the other cool stuff associated with 3D graphics.


While I was at it, I also learnt about the various line drawing algorithms, one of which I implemented which was pretty cool!
